Stock Markets Movement Prediction and Equity Price Relationships with Graph Neural Network (or just Neural Network)

Project Description

Investing in stock markets takes a lot in consideration, such as tax, trading costs, and risk management generally. With this nature, it is difficult to consider how much we can benefit from a single transaction without finance knowledge. This project aims to

  1. predict up/down for the equity, not considering how much they would change at the first place, and
  2. focus on a specific industry, or even for a few companies in the industry since it is generally difficult to build a universal model for all stock markets prediction.

Project Goals

  1. Predict the possible stock movement and its effect onto the other stocks in the industry
